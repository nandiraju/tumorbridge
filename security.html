How does TumorBridge handle data privacy and security

TumorBridge ensures data privacy and security by adhering to industry best practices and leveraging advanced technologies. Here are the key measures it employs:

  1. Encryption:

    • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols like TLS and X.509 certificates to safeguard sensitive information[9].

  2. HIPAA Compliance:

    • TumorBridge applies HIPAA-compliant systems to protect health data, ensuring adherence to strict legal and regulatory standards for patient privacy[9].

  3. Privacy by Design:

    • TumorBridge incorporates privacy by design principles, limiting data collection to only what is necessary and providing users with clear options for data sharing and consent[9].

  4. Data Anonymization:

    • Any research or development involving patient data is conducted on de-identified datasets, ensuring that personal information remains confidential unless explicit consent is provided[9].

  5. Access Control:

    • Strict internal access-control policies prevent unauthorized access to sensitive data, ensuring that only authorized personnel can handle patient information[9].

  6. Compliance with Global Privacy Standards:

    • TumorBridge aligns with international privacy laws such as GDPR, ensuring transparency in data handling and providing users with rights to access, modify, or delete their personal information[10].

  7. Blockchain Integration (Optional):

    • For enhanced security, TumorBridge may integrate blockchain technology to create immutable records of data modifications and manage user consent through smart contracts[2].
